info About Free Water Tips

Water is a right, not a product.

Our Mission

The UN believes that water is a fundamental human right and should not be treated as a commodity.

Our mission is to make it easier for people to find places that serve safe drinking water not burdened by a product or service tax, promoting sustainability and reducing plastic waste.

Why This Matters
